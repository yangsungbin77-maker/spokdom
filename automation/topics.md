# Publishing topic queue (English)

The auto-publish system pulls the first `- [ ]` item from the top and turns it into one post.
Published items become `- [x]`. Add new topics below in `- [ ]` format.
**Sort order: winnability score** (search volume counts logarithmically; lower competition and more
specific long-tail keywords go higher). A new domain ranks on long-tail buy-intent keywords first,
not head terms.

## Niche

Non-YMYL reviews and comparisons of AI tools and SaaS apps. Every post should help a reader who is
about to pay for software decide. Favor four buy-intent clusters: **alternatives**, **X vs Y**,
**pricing/plans**, and **best tool for [specific use case / solo / small team]**. Prefer programs
with recurring (monthly) affiliate commissions.
